The Problem: The AI Data Integrity Crisis

1. Unverifiable Dataset Lineage

AI models are trained on datasets with unknown provenance, exposing developers to severe copyright infringements, licensing violations, and data manipulation risks.

2. Centralized Bias & Compliance Checks

Current trust verification models depend on opaque centralized auditors. AI readiness, bias verification, and HIPAA/GDPR regulatory compliance checks remain a black box.

The Market Gap: AI needs a decentralized mechanism to verify training data quality, handle licensing peer-to-peer securely, and audit model checkpoints.

Core Innovation & Features

1. 9-Node Provenance Graph

An interactive, auditable trajectory illustrating the entire lifecycle of AI training data on-chain: from raw medical provider data down to commercial product royalties.

2. Triple-Index Trust Rating

Real-time client-side checks evaluating datasets side-by-side on **Dataset Trust Score**, **AI Readiness Index**, and **GDPR/HIPAA Compliance** indicators.

3. Secure AI Workspace

A training telemetry dashboard enabling researchers to run containerized notebook jobs and export model weights to mint on-chain checkpoint NFTs.

4. Checked Model Registry

A catalog system that binds compiled machine learning model files directly to their specific training data dependencies, preventing training audit spoofing.

Step-by-Step Project Workflow

1
Secure Upload & Scan: The dataset creator uploads training files. The platform checks formatting, audits GDPR compliance, and scores readiness.
2
IPFS Lock & Mint: Metadata is pinned to Pinata IPFS, and the Cardano ledger mints a verifiably unique Dataset Certificate NFT.
3
Escrow Locking: Buyers acquire access. The smart contract locks the purchase ADA, minting a CIP-25 License NFT to the buyer.
4
Model Training & Checkpointing: The buyer mounts the data in the Secure Workspace, trains their model, and exports weights as a cryptographic NFT.
5
Model Registration: The model is recorded in the Registry, permanently documenting its dataset dependency path for audit review.

Plutus Smart Escrow & Platform Economics

Cryptographic Escrow Rules

Contracts validate inputs and outputs on the transaction ledger. Direct peer-to-peer distribution ensures dataset sellers receive funds immediately upon buyer license minting, eliminating middlemen.

5% Governance Treasury Split

Every transaction routes **95% of licensing revenues** directly to the data provider, and **5% to the platform treasury**. The treasury funds are unlocked via community voting rules.

Community Governance Tab

ADA token holders vote on platform proposals to manage verification criteria and allocate treasury rewards securely using their own stake keys.
